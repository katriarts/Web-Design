Austria Clan

Ever wonder what is it like to have an extended family? or what is it like to be reunitedd with your family and other relatives in every special occassions?
Well, in all honesty, it is quite chaotic... but is really FUN!
The truth is, being the eldest chid in my part of nuclear family is very quiet. And I like it... A lot.
Not a lot of children to take care of for my two younger brothers are quite reliable, even if we fight some times, because I think that it's normal for siblings to fight... Because no matter what happens, we still have to be there... Alongside one another... Amazing, Right?

Back when I was still a kid, every holidays, we would go visit our province, and it was such an exciting feeling for me to always meet my cousins and friends at our province.
Playing with them under the sun, not caring about being scolded by our parents or other elderlies, I was such a reckless and sleepless child back then...
and I must say, I have enjoyed my childhood...

But as to what I have noticed, as I grew older, my cousins and friends grew apart, becoming too busy.
Even I don't socialize that much anymore, although, I'm not really a sociable person, they were usually the ones who initiate a friendship or a greeting.
It's not that I can't find time to see them, I'm just too busy focusing on my studies. One thing about me that my family had noticed is that I like studying, and yes, I do.
I like studying, because it makes my mind busy, in times of boredom or overthinking (because of panic attacks). But not because I like to study doesn't mean that I am very intellectual.
I'm heck a procrastinator too. It's quite hard to explain, but... when I feel lazy, I would finish all my requirements so I can finally procrastinate. I'm not that smart.

Wished I could go to our province again. I'd love to meet the kids (the millennial ones), I kind of like children, but npot into taking care of them because I'm a childish bum.

Junior High Friends

My Junior High School years have been splendid for an introverted person like me, it's because my friends are also introverts.
I didn't even remember how we've became the best of friends. I just know that we've got quite a lot in common.
It doesn't get awkward when we're walking and not even talking with one another.

Even though we've went in our separate ways now that we are in college, we still try to contact each other, and chatting them how are they.
I do miss them, and I wish them success, and maybe one day, we'll all get together, and have some bonding time, like we used to do.

Senior High in UC

Senior High School, my first time in the University of the Cordilleras... being a lone wolf.
Then, suddenly, people approaching me, making new friends... I feeel like my introverted self is becoming social...
but still... a socially-awkward little dumpling am I.

It's weird but, the truth is, I feel quite uncomfortable. I started to realize that my social interaction is improving.
I got to consider myself and even declared to myself that I am an ambivert.

But it's just what I thought. I know that I have these kind of classmates who tries to talk to me or walk with me or even asking me out or joining on their hangouts and such...
But I got so anxious, panic attacks and sensitiveness, I hate it. I'm not an emo or anything, I'm just too self-conscious...
I can't even tell it to anyone... The feelings... It just... comes and go... That's why I never told anyone...
I remained silent... Hiding... Smiling... and trying to have fun... I'm trying...

I consider the people who tells me the truth face-to-face about what they don't like about me as mannered people.
Expecially when they make me cry... I know it is weird, but they were so truthful that I want to be friends with them...
Even if I have a sensitive soul.
